Frequently Asked Questions

Answers from TrumpRx.gov.

For Patients
TrumpRx is a program that brings Most-Favored-Nation pricing to prescription drugs in America. The same medications, now at the lowest prices available in the developed world, benchmarked against international reference prices.
Currently 43 medications are listed, including GLP-1 drugs like Wegovy, Ozempic, and Zepbound, along with a range of other commonly prescribed medications. More are being added over time. Browse the full list.
You can browse prices without an account. To purchase medications you need a valid prescription. TrumpRx provides coupon codes (BIN, PCN, Group, Member ID) to present at participating pharmacies.
TrumpRx coupons are widely supported at retail pharmacies nationwide. Check individual drug detail pages on TrumpRx.gov for specific pharmacy availability.
The service itself is free. You only pay the listed price for your medication. No hidden fees.
TrumpRx pricing is available regardless of insurance status. However, you cannot seek insurance reimbursement for out-of-pocket costs for prescriptions purchased with TrumpRx coupons. Participants must not be enrolled in government-funded programs (Medicare, Medicaid, VA, TRICARE).
Some medications may have different packaging due to international sourcing, but they contain the same active ingredients and dosages. Actual product appearance may vary from images shown on TrumpRx.gov.
For Prescribers & Pharmacies
Prescriptions are electronically routed through the network of participating pharmacies using standard ePrescribing protocols. Some medications (like Zepbound) are managed directly through the manufacturer (e.g., LillyDirect).
Claims are processed at the listed TrumpRx price using the provided coupon credentials (BIN: 015995, PCN: GDC, Group: MAHA). Pharmacies receive payment directly through the network.
TrumpRx pricing is designed around Most-Favored-Nation provisions, benchmarking against international reference prices. Visit TrumpRx.gov for current compliance details.

Stay Updated

New medications are being added. Visit TrumpRx.gov to get notified when more drugs are available at MFN pricing.
